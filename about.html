The Journey

56 Years of Building Kerala's Workforce

1968
Founded in Palluruthy, Kochi

M.S. Prakash Panicker establishes Bluetronix with a mission: give practical technical skills to Kerala's youth. At a time when vocational training was overlooked, he saw its massive potential. The institute begins with a handful of students and a conviction that hands-on learning changes lives.

1975
ITES Affiliation Granted

Received official ITES affiliation (Order No. ITES/106/75/4/9/75-KRL/263), cementing Bluetronix as a recognized vocational institution under the Government of India — one of the first private ITIs in Kerala to achieve this.

1978
Kerala Government Recognition

Government of Kerala formally recognizes Bluetronix via G.O. No. C5/45254/77/KDIS.TVM dated 28/02/1978. This recognition opens the door to government-affiliated certifications and wider student access across Ernakulam.

1987
DGE&T National Affiliation

Affiliated with the Directorate General of Employment & Training, Ministry of Labour, Government of India. Our certificates now carry national weight — opening doors to government jobs, defence, Railways and international Gulf opportunities for every graduate.

2001
Expanded Trade Affiliations

New trades added and affiliations expanded under DGE&T letter No. C4/23465/2001. Bluetronix grows from a single-trade institute to a comprehensive multi-trade ITI serving the diverse skill needs of Kochi and surrounding districts.

2020
NCVET Integration — Skill India

Bluetronix integrates fully with the NCVET-MIS national portal (MIS Code: PU32000240). All certifications are now verifiable via QR code — tamper-proof, globally traceable credentials that employers and governments trust.

Today
56 Years Strong. Still Growing.

Under Mrs. Usha P. Panicker, Bluetronix continues its mission. Companies call us directly seeking fresh graduates. Our pass-outs work in the Gulf, Indian Railways, ISRO, and run their own businesses across Kerala. The best chapters are ahead.

Education Pathway

Your ITI is Just the Beginning

After completing your ITI, multiple doors open. You can enter the workforce immediately, or continue studying through lateral entry — reaching a B.Tech degree in just 5 years total from 10th standard.

Direct admission to 2nd year Polytechnic Diploma via lateral entry.

B.Tech Degree

Lateral entry to B.Tech after Diploma. Full Engineering Degree in your hands.
