An ITI qualification is not a consolation prize — it is a direct, government-certified route to skilled employment. While a four-year college degree offers theory, an ITI gives you practice, a nationally-recognized certificate, and a job on day one.

01
Practical Training on Real Equipment

At Bluetronix, students work on actual diesel engines, AC compressors and electrical installations — not textbook diagrams. This hands-on approach builds genuine competence that employers recognise and value.

02
Direct Employment Connections

Companies contact Bluetronix directly seeking our graduates. Our placement record speaks to the quality of training we provide. Students are job-ready from the day they complete their course.

03
Government & Defence Sector Eligibility

Holders of the National Trade Certificate are eligible to apply for positions in Indian Railways, the Army, Airforce, PWD, ISRO, and ONGC — opportunities unavailable to many degree holders.

04
Open to All — No Age Limit

SSLC pass required. There is no upper age limit. Bluetronix welcomes anyone committed to learning a skilled trade, regardless of their academic history.

Est. 1968
"Where ever you go — tell that you are a Bluetronix product, and feel the change of gravity in your personality."

Bluetronix was founded in 1968 by M.S. Prakash Panicker on ESI Road, Palluruthy, Kochi. From its earliest days, the institution held a singular conviction: that a young person equipped with genuine practical skill will always find their place in the world, regardless of their academic record.

Over five decades later, that conviction has been vindicated thousands of times over. Bluetronix graduates work in the Gulf, in Indian Railways, in ISRO, in shipyards and in their own businesses across Kerala. Today, the institution is led by Mrs. Usha P. Panicker, who continues to build on a foundation of rigorous, practical, employment-focused training.
