Common Questions

Parents & Students
Ask Us Everything

Admission-ന് എന്ത് qualification വേണം? +

SSLC (10th Standard) pass — that's all. No minimum percentage, no entrance exam. No upper age limit either — whether you're 16 or 40, you are eligible. Boys and girls both welcome across all trades.

Girls-നും join ചെയ്യാൻ പറ്റുമോ? +

Yes, absolutely. All five NCVT trades — AC & Refrigeration, Electrician, Diesel Mechanic, Draughtsman Civil, and Fitter — are open to both boys and girls equally. No restrictions.

Certificate-ന് India മൊത്തം valid ആണോ? +

Yes — and internationally too. Bluetronix issues the National Trade Certificate (NTC) awarded by DGT, Ministry of Labour, Government of India. It is valid across India and recognised in Gulf countries, Europe, and beyond. Every certificate carries a QR code for instant online verification.

Admission എപ്പോൾ തുടങ്ങും? +

The academic session starts every August. Only 21 seats per trade are available — so applications fill up quickly. Submit your enquiry now to secure your seat for August 2025.

Admission-ന് എന്ത് documents വേണം? +

Bring the following at the time of admission:

• SSLC original + 3 photocopies
• Original Transfer Certificate (TC)
• Caste certificate (SC/ST candidates)
• 3 passport-size photos (white background)
• Copy of Aadhaar card

Parent or guardian must accompany the candidate.

Course കഴിഞ്ഞ് ജോലി ഉറപ്പാണോ? +

We don't just promise — companies call us. Bluetronix runs a compulsory On-Job Training (OJT) programme. Our placement partners — Blue Star, Voltas, Crompton, SFO Technologies, Maruti Suzuki and others — contact us directly asking for trained students. Graduates are job-ready from day one.

ITI കഴിഞ്ഞ് degree എടുക്കാൻ പറ്റുമോ? +

Yes — full B.Tech is possible. After ITI → Polytechnic Diploma via lateral entry (2 years) → B.Tech via lateral entry (3 years). A full engineering degree in just 5–6 years from 10th, at a fraction of the cost of a private engineering college.

Hostel facility ഉണ്ടോ? +

Yes — for outstation students. Hostel accommodation can be arranged for students coming from outside Kochi. All expenses are to be met by the student. Contact the office for current availability and rates.

Gulf-ൽ ജോലി കിട്ടുമോ? +

Yes — high demand, especially for AC & Electrician. The National Trade Certificate is internationally recognised. Gulf countries — UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait — actively recruit ITI-qualified technicians. AC Refrigeration and Electrician trades have the highest Gulf demand.

Army / Navy / ISRO-ൽ apply ചെയ്യാൻ ITI മതിയോ? +

Yes — directly eligible. NTC holders can apply to Indian Railways, Indian Army, Indian Navy (Artificer Apprentice), Indian Air Force, PWD, ISRO Technician-B, and ONGC — positions that are not available to many degree holders. Fitter and Electrician trades have the strongest government recruitment.
